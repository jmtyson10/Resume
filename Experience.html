GIS Technician

I started working as a GIS Technician 5 years ago at a water treatment and distribution company. This company did not have a GIS program and did not have GIS data, workflows, and minimal equipment. This afforded me a unique opportunity to be able to build a program/department from the bottom up.

  • Database Management: I have developed two and managed three geospatial databases. I built links between all three to be able to produce useful visualizations saving the company time and money. I ensured data quality by developing specific workflows for data collection.
  • Technologies: I taught myself how to use and integrate different platforms to increase geospatial productivity including ArcGIS PRO, Collector, WorkForce, SEDARU, Tremble R2 and R1 GNSSs, across different operating systems such as iOS and Android.
  • Python and SQL: I increased efficiency by writing scripts and tools to query data and automate tasks/processes using SQL and Python. I have also used Python to clean, analyze and visualize data for pattern recognition and report building.
